## The Symbiosis of FTTH and Supportive Technologies: A  Lifeline for Seniors

Fibre-To-The-Home (FTTH) connectivity acts as a  catalyst for supportive technologies in elderly care. High-speed internet empowers telehealth services, enabling remote monitoring and timely interventions for  seniors. Virtual Reality (VR), when combined with FTTH, amplifies cognitive stimulation experiences, preserving  cognitive abilities. Smart assistive devices connected via FTTH  provide seamless monitoring and assistance  addressing the unique needs of seniors. FTTH bridges the gap fostering social connectivity and  virtual companionship, alleviating the scourge of social isolation among the elderly.

## AI Care  Companions: A  New Era  of Personalized Assistance

Intuition  Robotics, a leading innovator in AI care companionship, has  garnered significant attention for its ElliQ robot. ElliQ employs generative  AI and  Large Language Models  (LLMs) to  engage seniors in continuous conversational experiences. This remarkable device performs health check-ins, promotes physical and cognitive activities and encourages social connections. Intuition  Robotics is dedicated to expanding ElliQ's capabilities and meeting the growing demand from healthcare organizations  and government agencies.

The integration  of FTTH and supportive technologies  empowers real-time health monitoring and safety for seniors.  Robotics and AI-powered companions provide physical assistance and emotional support contributing to the well-being of elderly individuals. This synergy has the potential to transform elderly care, leading to a more fulfilling and  vibrant life for seniors.
